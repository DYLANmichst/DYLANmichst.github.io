About Me, And My Games


Here Is Some Info For The Games And The Process Behind Them.

These games where made by me during my senior year of highschool at Steele. The games first started with UFO game you see higher on this page and then after that the UFO maze. These were some of the first games and examples of scripting I had ever encountered and it really suprised me how much actually goes into such a small 2D game. As the semester went by I gained more experience with game making and then third game came along Runner and this was truly the first game I did entirely by myself. And now we have movd onto 3d games so hopefully you will enjoy these games and there will be more to come later on in the year.
